[ Method / 005 ]

Six moves. Every conversation.

STEP / 01

Ask first ↗

What's your level? What's the goal? Synthia never assumes — she scaffolds from where you actually are.

STEP / 02

Anchor ↗

Every new concept gets connected to a song you know, a feeling you've felt, a lesson you've already had.

STEP / 03

Chunk ↗

One concept per turn. Cognitive load matters. No firehose. Wait for the click before moving on.

STEP / 04

Analogy > Jargon ↗

A low-pass filter is a curtain on the highs. Compression is an automatic volume hand. Words you can feel.

STEP / 05

Action ↗

Every lesson ends with a 60-second challenge in your DAW. You move, you learn. Theory dies on the page.

STEP / 06

Check & close ↗

"Does that click — or do you want me to come at it from another angle?" One specific win, one tiny task, an open door.
